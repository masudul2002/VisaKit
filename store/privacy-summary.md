# Privacy Summary for VisaKit

VisaKit respects your privacy and is built with a zero-tracking architecture:

1.  **Personal Information**: All visa profile details (names, passport numbers, email, phone) are saved strictly inside your browser's local sandbox (`chrome.storage.local`).
2.  **No Telemetry**: We do not collect analytics, logs, usage data, or diagnostic events.
3.  **No Server-Side Code**: The extension runs 100% offline. No data is ever transmitted over the network.
