# VisaKit — Secure Indian Visa Autofill

Simplify your visa application process with VisaKit, the browser extension designed to help you prepare and complete your online Indian Visa Application forms (indianvisaonline.gov.in) quickly and securely.

---

## ✨ Features

*   **One-Click Autofill**: Automatically maps your saved applicant details to input fields, textareas, dropdown selections, radio buttons, and dates on the official portal.
*   **Sandbox Security**: All your data is saved locally on your computer inside your browser's Chrome Storage Sandbox.
*   **Zero Remote Calls**: VisaKit never sends your personal information, passport details, or configurations to external servers or cloud networks.
*   **Backups Management**: Export profiles as an encrypted JSON backup file and import them back at any time.

---

## 🔒 Security & Policy Boundaries

VisaKit does NOT bypass portal checks, interact with CAPTCHAs, or send automated form submissions. It is designed to assist you in entering details, leaving security checkpoints for you to complete manually.
